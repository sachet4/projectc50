How to make a camel?

Some interesting facts about camels

Camel have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes

Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms

Camels can travel at uo to 40 miles per hour - the same as a racehorse!

How to make a chameleon?

Some interesting facts about chameleons

Smaller chameleons have faster tongues

Their eyes can swivel around in two different directions simultaneously

They mainly change their colour in order to communicate or regulate body temperature

How to make a flying cicada?

Some interesting facts about cicada

Most of the cicadas have red-orange eyes.But occasionally cicadas have blue,whiteor grayish eyes.

Female cicadas may be attracted to the sound of motors.

They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil. When they die, their rotting bodies put nitrogen into the soil.

How to make a panda?

Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.

An adult panda can eat 12-38 kilos of Bamboo per day!
